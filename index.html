The Artist

Colour, pattern, and rhythm in every bead

Tinashe Kachama is the founder of Paivepo Art & Decor, based at Old Nick Village in Plettenberg Bay. Rooted in African heritage and inspired by nature, music, and tradition, his beaded sculptures carry memory, culture, and soul. Each piece is thoughtfully designed — a meditative act of creation that can take weeks to complete.

The Artist

Tinashe Kachama
Beadwork Sculptor

Rooted in African heritage, Tinashe Kachama creates beaded sculptures and fine art that carry memory, culture, and soul. He founded Paivepo Art & Decor in 2021 and is based at Old Nick Village in Plettenberg Bay, where his studio is open every day of the week.

The name Paivepo — a Shona word meaning "There was" — is the way his late grandmother would begin her stories. "The moment she said it, our eyes would widen and we'd brace ourselves for whatever tale was coming next." That same sense of anticipation, of a story about to unfold, lives in every piece Tinashe makes.

The Process

A dance of
imagination and precision

01
Conceptualise

It begins with an idea, often inspired by elements of African culture or nature. Tinashe sketches patterns and shapes that feel right. He studies the animal — its posture, movement, the quality of its presence — before a single bead is placed.

02
Select & Feel

"Selecting the beads is an emotional part. Every colour tells something." The choice of palette is intuitive — guided by the creature's nature, the story being told, and the spirit Tinashe intends the finished piece to carry.

03
Thread & Refine

Then comes the slow, meditative threading process — bead by bead, in quiet focus. Traditional African rhythms play while Tinashe works. "Each sculpture holds a heartbeat." The final stages are about refining the piece until it carries exactly the spirit intended.

Commission

Create something entirely yours

"Commissions allow me to dig deeper — to make something that speaks directly to someone's journey." Whether it is a beloved animal, a meaningful symbol, or a centrepiece for a special space, Tinashe takes on a select number of custom commissions.
